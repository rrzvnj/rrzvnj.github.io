About Me

I am a hobbyist photographer. I enjoy taking pictures from nature, city sites, or anything that I find interesting. I love traveling and I usually take photos while I am on a trip. However, I have never been on any dedicated photography trips. I am a self-learner photographer and with each photo I take, I try to improve my skills. The photos that are on this website are not edited. Apart from this website, I also upload my photos to my Instagram channel. You can find a link to my channel down below. I hope you enjoy your visit while you go through my Photo Gallery.
